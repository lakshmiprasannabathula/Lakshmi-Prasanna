Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps to Cook

  2. Chop tomatoes, onion, and garlic.

  4. Add tomatoes and cook into a sauce.
